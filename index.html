Flow-Control Decision Software

Ferrum fuses deterministic workflows with targeted AI. You design decision flows visually. We compile them into bulletproof, auditable execution on Redis.

What flows do:

  • Route decisions through typed, validated objects
  • Enforce business rules with mathematical precision
  • Insert AI where it adds value (classification, ranking, extraction)
  • Generate complete audit trails automatically
  • Adapt without redeployment

What flows avoid:

  • Black-box neural networks making binary decisions
  • Hallucination-prone LLMs writing code or logic
  • Vendor lock-in (runs on your infrastructure)
  • Months of integration work
