Credit Decisioning

Real-time, auditable underwriting decisions.

The Problem

You have a credit policy: income thresholds, DTI ratios, FICO cutoffs. But the model outputs "score: 0.67." Okay. Now what?

What the Regulator Says

"Tell me why you denied this customer. Cite the rule. Show the logic." But your model doesn't cite rules. You don't have logic. You have a number.

The Ferrum Approach

Rules first. AI optional. Complete trace of every decision.

Result

Auditable decisions in under 200 ms. Logged to Redis. Queryable in real time. Ready for the examiner.

Claims Triage

Classify and route claims without bottlenecks.

The Problem

Claims flood in. They're classified by ML. Routed by static rules. Reviewed manually. Every step is a latency bomb.

Why Workflow Matters

AI can't tell you which claims are high-priority unless you define what high-priority means. And when that definition changes, you need to update the workflow—not retrain the model.

The Ferrum Approach

Triage logic in visual flows. AI for classification. Redis Streams for routing. Sub-second triage to the right queue.

Result

Claims reach adjusters faster. Complexity handled by logic—not luck.

Compliance & KYC Automation

Execute compliance logic with full traceability.

The Problem

"We use AI to flag bad actors." Great. What's the policy? "We trained on historical data." What are the thresholds? "Umm…"

The Regulatory Reality

AML/KYC rules are explicit. The logic must be testable. Reproducible. Explainable. This isn't "nice to have." It's table stakes.

The Ferrum Approach

Compliance logic runs as deterministic flows. AI enriches (e.g., entity extraction), but rules decide.

Result

Audit logs with full decision rationale. Regulator-ready. Model-agnostic.

Content Moderation

Enforce policy with rules—not just AI predictions.

The Problem

Why This Matters

Platform trust is eroded when moderation is opaque. Users challenge decisions. Regulators question enforcement. You need to show your work.

The Ferrum Approach

Rules first: keyword blocklists, context rules, AI for edge cases. Escalation flows for appeals.

Result

Transparent, contestable moderation. Faster response. Cleaner platform.

Result

Tasks land in the right queue. First time. Every time.
